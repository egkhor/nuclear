The New Playbook

Four Steps to Deploy Nuclear Fast

01
Adopt Proven SMR Technologies
No reinvention. Use battle-tested SMR designs from GE Hitachi, Westinghouse. Let AI accelerate validation, not starting from zero.
02
Integrate AI into Design & Validation
Deploy AI simulation and digital twin workflows across design, safety modeling, and regulatory submissions from Day 1.
03
Deploy Modular Units Across States
Build multiple SMRs at different sites simultaneously — distributed energy security, not centralized dependency.
04
Execute in Parallel, Not Sequentially
Run design, regulatory, and site preparation concurrently. Sequential is the old model. Parallel execution is how you hit 5–7 years.
